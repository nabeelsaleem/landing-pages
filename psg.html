FAQ

GOT QUESTIONS?

What materials are used in PSG paddles? +
All PSG paddles use FT-700 unidirectional raw carbon fiber face surfaces, polypropylene honeycomb cores, and aerospace-grade edge guard composites. Each material is selected for its contribution to performance — no filler, no compromises.
Are PSG paddles approved for tournaments? +
Yes. All PSG paddles in the Strike Pro and Apex Elite series are USA Pickleball Association (USAPA) approved and listed on the official approved paddle list. You can play with confidence at any sanctioned event.
What is your return policy? +
We offer a 30-day no-questions-asked return policy. If you play it and don't love it, ship it back for a full refund or exchange. Pro and Elite bundle purchases include free return shipping labels.
How do I choose the right paddle weight? +
Generally, lighter paddles (7.0–7.8oz) offer better maneuverability and are easier on joints — great for beginners or net play specialists. Heavier paddles (8.0–8.5oz) provide more power and stability, favored by baseline players. If in doubt, start mid-range with the Pulse Core 14.
Do you ship internationally? +
We currently ship to the USA, Canada, UK, and Australia with flat-rate international shipping. We're expanding rapidly — sign up to our newsletter to be notified when we launch in your region.
