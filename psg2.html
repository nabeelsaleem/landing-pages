What makes PSG paddles different?+
PSG paddles use FT-700 unidirectional raw carbon fiber — the same material class found in aerospace applications. Combined with our proprietary hexagonal honeycomb core geometry and Shock Arrestor edge guard, you get performance that rivals paddles costing 2–3x more.
Are these paddles USAPA tournament approved?+
Yes. The Strike Pro 16, Apex Elite, and Pulse Core 14 are all USAPA approved and appear on the official approved paddle list. You can compete with confidence in any sanctioned tournament at any level.
What is your 30-day trial policy?+
Play it for 30 days. If you don't love it for any reason, return it for a full refund. No restocking fees, no questions asked. Pro and Elite bundle orders include a pre-paid return label — just box it up and drop it off.
Which paddle is right for my skill level?+
Beginners: Start with the Pulse Core 14 — its oversized sweet spot is extremely forgiving. Intermediate players: Strike Pro 16 gives the best balance of power and control. Advanced/Pro: Apex Elite for maximum spin and precision. Youth: Volt Junior is purpose-built for players ages 10–17.
Do you offer a warranty?+
All PSG paddles come with a minimum 1-year warranty against manufacturing defects. Elite bundle purchases include a lifetime warranty. Normal wear and edge chips from rim strikes are covered under our Shock Arrestor guarantee for 6 months.
