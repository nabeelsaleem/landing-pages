How It Works

Intelligence at Every Checkpoint

Three unified modules. One operations layer — from check-in to wheels-up.

01

Baggage Detection

Real-time CV models classify every bag at check-in and belt entry — size, type, compliance — in under 200ms.

02

03

📷

Baggage Detection Engine

Multi-angle IP cameras feed our edge inference models in real time. No cloud round-trip. No misses.

Multi-angle IP cameras capture every bag at belt entry
Edge AI classifies size, type, and policy compliance instantly
CV cross-references bag tags with passenger manifests
Staff receive instant mobile alerts for oversize or restricted items
🚶

Boarding Optimizer

AI forecasts bin utilisation, re-sequences passengers, and nudges them via app — all before gates open.

Forecast cabin & overhead bin utilisation 90 mins before gate open
Dynamically recalculate boarding order by seat map and bag load
Deliver personalised boarding-time nudges via airline app or display
Post-flight analytics benchmark and feed next-flight model updates
💰

Revenue Recovery Module

Combine booking data, loyalty history, and live queue context to score and convert every ancillary opportunity.

AI combines booking data, loyalty status, and real-time queue context
Score each passenger for upgrade, bag fee, and lounge upsell
Trigger personalised offers to gate agents or passenger mobile
Accepted and declined offers retrain the model continuously
